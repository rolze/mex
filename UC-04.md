9. `Esc` (1st) clears the selection; (2nd) closes preview; (3rd) clears filter.

**Selection model:**
- `Space` toggles individual files independently.
- Shift-Up/Down: first press (or direction change) toggles current + landed; continuing same direction only toggles landed.
- Shift-Home/End: independent toggle-range per key press.
